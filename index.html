Alternate PyBank and PyPoll Solutions

The three Pybank solutions demonstrate good coding practices
for comments and clarity. They also show the difference between an
iterative one-at-a_time style vs. an
iterator do-it-all-at-once style.


The PyPoll solution uses a dictionary rather than a list to show
why dictionaries are often the preferred data structure as well
as introducing students to the idiomatic or 'Pythonic' philosophy of
coding.
